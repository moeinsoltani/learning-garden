# Phase 3: TLS & SSL

The protocol that secures almost everything. From the SSL→TLS history through the 1.2 and 1.3
handshakes, cipher suites and forward secrecy, mutual TLS, the extensions that make the modern
web work (SNI, ALPN, resumption, 0-RTT), how to inspect and harden a deployment, and the famous
attacks that shaped today's defaults.

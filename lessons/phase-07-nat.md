# Phase 7: NAT, Conntrack & DHCP

This phase recreates a home router: NAT to share one public IP, connection tracking to make
NAT and stateful firewalling possible, and DHCP to hand out addresses automatically. Together
they're the machinery behind almost every gateway you've ever used.

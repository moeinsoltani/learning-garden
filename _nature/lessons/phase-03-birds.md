# Phase 3: Birds of Canada

The birds — grouped by *where you meet them*, which is exactly how a beginner's
brain files them. First the **backyard and city birds** at your feeder and in
the park; then the **water birds** on every cottage lake and city pond; then the
**raptors** overhead; then the **forest birds** you hear before you see; and
finally the **iconic** few that show up on coins, stamps, and the national
imagination.

Each lesson pairs a clear photo of every species with the two or three field
marks that pin it down, so you can tell a chickadee from a nuthatch and a raven
from a crow.

# Phase 4: Seasons, Habitats & Field Skills

Trees and birds are not a static poster; they are a calendar. This short final
phase puts the species back into the turning year — who flies south, who toughs
out the Canadian winter, and how a maple, a chickadee, and a loon each mark the
seasons — and then sends you outside with a **capstone walk** that asks you to
read a whole scene at once and put everything together.

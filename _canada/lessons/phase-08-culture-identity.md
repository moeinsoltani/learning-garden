# Phase 8: Culture & Identity

The capstone phase and its question: what, if anything, is a Canadian?
Official bilingualism's policy and reality, multiculturalism — the
world's first official version — and its debates, Indigenous Canada as
present-tense nations, the symbols and holidays and their decoding,
the shared culture from hockey to CanCon to Cohen, and the final
synthesis lesson where you write your own answer to the identity
question and take the cumulative citizenship-style exam.

Every lesson's lab is a **source & scenario exercise** with hidden
model answers; checkpoints are citizenship-test-style questions. All
seven previous phases are assumed — this phase is where their threads
braid.

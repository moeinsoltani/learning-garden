# Phase 5: Government & Institutions

How the machine actually works — the phase that turns Canadian news from
noise into signal. The Crown and the constitutional monarchy, Parliament's
two chambers and how a bill becomes law, where power really sits (the PM,
cabinet, and confidence), elections and what first-past-the-post does,
federalism's who-does-what, the courts and Canada's two legal traditions,
and the Charter in action — including the two clauses that make Canadian
rights different.

Every lesson's lab is a **source & scenario exercise** with hidden model
answers; checkpoints are citizenship-test-style questions. History phases
2–4 are assumed throughout — every institution here is an artifact of a
bargain you've now seen struck.

# Phase 6: Civic Life

Citizenship as it's actually lived: what citizenship formally means and
how it's acquired, what happens when the law meets daily life (police,
courts, jury duty), the social safety net from birth to retirement,
where the public money comes from and goes, and how to actually take
part — voting at three levels, contacting representatives, and civic
participation.

Every lesson's lab is a **source & scenario exercise** with hidden model
answers; checkpoints are citizenship-test-style questions — this phase
in particular doubles as direct citizenship-test preparation.

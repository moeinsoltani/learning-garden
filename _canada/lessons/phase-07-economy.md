# Phase 7: The Economy

What Canada does for a living, and why the US relationship dominates
everything: the economy's real shape (services vs resources vs the
self-image), the elephant next door (trade, USMCA, the forever
disputes), the flagship industries and where they live, the Bank of
Canada and the deliberately boring banks, and the labour market a
newcomer or citizen actually navigates.

Every lesson's lab is a **source & scenario exercise** with hidden model
answers; checkpoints are citizenship-test-style questions. Phase 1's
resource map and Phase 5's federalism are assumed throughout.

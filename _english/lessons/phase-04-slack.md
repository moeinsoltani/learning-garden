# Phase 4: Slack Communication

With accurate (Phase 1), natural (Phase 2), and warm (Phase 3) writing as the
foundation, this phase applies it to the specific patterns of Slack — where most of
your work communication happens. It covers message shapes (lead with the ask),
status updates, asking for help well, answering and unblocking others, async
etiquette (the unwritten rules), and announcements to wide channels.

Rewrite-drill labs with real Slack-style examples and phrase banks.

# Phase 8: Feedback & Hard Conversations

This phase tackles the hardest communication a lead does: giving difficult feedback, writing
review comments that teach without stinging, having performance conversations, delivering bad
news, and de-escalating when emotions run high. These are exactly the moments where warmth and
clarity matter most — and where a non-native speaker most needs the right language, because the
stakes are high and there's little room to be misread.

The lessons give you concrete language for each: the words that make hard feedback land as care
rather than attack, the phrasings that deliver bad news honestly but kindly, and the moves that
cool a heated moment. Built on the warmth foundation (Phase 3) and the disagreement skills
(Lessons 18, 30), now applied to the highest-stakes conversations.

The labs are rewrite drills on real hard-conversation moments, with phrase banks of tested lines.

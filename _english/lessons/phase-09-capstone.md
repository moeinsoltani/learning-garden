# Phase 9: Capstone & Habits

The final phase turns everything you've learned into *lasting improvement*. Knowledge fades
without practice, so these three lessons build the habits that keep you improving after the course
ends: a personal error checklist tuned to your own recurring mistakes, a repeatable rewrite-
practice routine, and a set of small daily habits that make better communication automatic.

This is where the course becomes permanent — not a thing you read once, but a practice you carry
forward. The labs help you build your *own* tools (your error list, your practice routine), so the
improvement continues under your own steam.

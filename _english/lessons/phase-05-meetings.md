# Phase 5: Meetings (Speaking)

Phases 1–4 built accurate, natural, warm *writing*. This phase shifts to *speaking* —
the meetings where much of your influence as a lead happens. It covers setting agendas
and opening a meeting, facilitating so everyone contributes, interrupting and clarifying
gracefully, disagreeing live (in real time, without a chance to edit), summarizing and
capturing action items, presenting your work, and the small talk that builds rapport.

The labs stay rewrite-and-say drills: broken or blunt spoken lines, better versions, and
phrase banks of same-day-usable meeting language — so you have the words ready when it's
your turn to speak.

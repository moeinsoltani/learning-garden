# Phase 2: Words That Sound Natural

Phase 1 made your sentences accurate. This phase makes them sound *natural* —
fixing the word choices that mark writing as non-native even when the grammar is
correct. It covers the verb pairs that trip everyone up (do/make, say/tell), the
phrasal verbs that dominate office English (follow up, roll out, push back),
sentences that are grammatically fine but structured like your first language, and
choosing plainer, more concise words.

Rewrite-drill labs with software-workplace examples and phrase banks, as before.

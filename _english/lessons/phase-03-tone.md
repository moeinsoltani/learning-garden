# Phase 3: Tone & Warmth

This is the phase that directly addresses your central goal: sounding **warm and
soft** rather than cold or blunt. With accurate sentences (Phase 1) and natural
word choice (Phase 2) as the foundation, this phase covers the tools that make
messages land as friendly as you intend: matching register to the audience,
softeners and hedging (the #1 warmth tool), warm openers and closers, requests
that don't sound like orders, disagreeing and saying no kindly, natural apologies
and thanks, and adding the engagement signals that text strips away.

Rewrite-drill labs with software-workplace examples and phrase banks throughout.

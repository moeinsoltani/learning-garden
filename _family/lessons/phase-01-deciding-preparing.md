# Phase 1: Deciding & Preparing

Before there's a bump, there's a decision — and a season of getting ready. This
phase covers the part most people skip: preparing your bodies, your health, and
your life *before* trying to conceive, when small choices (folic acid, vaccines,
quitting harmful habits, managing a health condition) have their biggest effect.
It starts with a bird's-eye view of the whole journey, then gets practical.

{: .important }
> General education, not medical advice — always follow your own doctor, OB, or
> midwife. When in doubt, call. (See the learning plan's note.)

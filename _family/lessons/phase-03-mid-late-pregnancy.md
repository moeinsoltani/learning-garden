# Phase 3: Mid & Late Pregnancy

Weeks 14 to birth: often the most enjoyable stretch (the second trimester),
followed by the heavy, expectant final months. This phase covers what happens in
each, the routine monitoring that keeps mother and baby safe, the main
complications to know about, the warning signs that mean "call now," and how to
stay physically and mentally well as the finish line approaches.

{: .important }
> General education, not medical advice — always follow your own doctor, OB, or
> midwife. When in doubt, call.

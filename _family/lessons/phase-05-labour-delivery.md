# Phase 5: Labour & Delivery

The big day. Birth is unpredictable, but understanding it removes a lot of fear.
This phase walks through the signs and stages of labour and when to go in, what a
vaginal birth actually involves, what happens in a C-section (planned or
unplanned), and the precious "golden hour" right after — skin-to-skin, the first
feed, and the partner's important role throughout.

{: .important }
> General education, not medical advice — always follow your own doctor, OB, or
> midwife. When in doubt, call.

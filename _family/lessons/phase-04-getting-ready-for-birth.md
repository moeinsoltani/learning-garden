# Phase 4: Getting Ready for Birth

The last stretch of preparation, when planning turns practical. This phase helps
you choose who will care for you and where you'll give birth, think through your
preferences for labour and pain relief, take the right classes and pack the bag,
and set up a safe, ready home — a car seat, a safe sleep space, and the handful
of things a newborn actually needs.

{: .important }
> General education, not medical advice — always follow your own doctor, OB, or
> midwife. When in doubt, call.

# Phase 6: The Newborn — The Fourth Trimester

The first weeks at home: tiny, wonderful, and relentless. This is the biggest
phase because it's where new parents have the most questions. It covers what
newborns can do and need, safe sleep, both kinds of feeding, everyday care,
soothing a crying baby (and the vital rule about never shaking one), and the
health basics and red flags every new parent must know.

{: .important }
> General education, not medical advice — always follow your own doctor, midwife,
> or your baby's provider. When in doubt, call. For a young baby, trust your
> instincts and don't wait.

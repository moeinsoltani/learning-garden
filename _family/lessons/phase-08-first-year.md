# Phase 8: The First Year

From a sleepy newborn to a babbling, crawling near-toddler — the first year is
astonishing. This final phase looks ahead: introducing solid foods, the
developmental milestones (and the red flags worth watching), how sleep evolves,
keeping a mobile baby healthy and safe in a baby-proofed home, and the big
practical transition of returning to work and arranging childcare — ending at the
first birthday.

{: .important }
> General education, not medical advice — always follow your own doctor or your
> baby's provider. When in doubt, call.

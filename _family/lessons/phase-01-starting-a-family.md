# Phase 1: Starting a Family

The beginning of the parenthood journey: deciding to try, and understanding the
whole path from *before* pregnancy, through the nine months, to the first weeks
after birth. The goal of this phase is simply to feel **prepared and unafraid** —
to know what matters, what to do early, and which signs mean "call someone now."

{: .important }
> General education, not medical advice — always follow your own doctor, OB, or
> midwife. See the learning plan's note.

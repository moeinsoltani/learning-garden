# Phase 7: Recovery & the New Family

A baby is born, but so is a family — and the parents need care too. This phase is
about the people often overlooked once the baby arrives: the mother's physical
recovery, everyone's mental health, the partner's essential role, the couple's
relationship under strain, and the practical survival skills of sleep
deprivation, visitors, and building a support network.

{: .important }
> General education, not medical advice — always follow your own doctor, OB, or
> midwife. Postpartum warning signs can appear for up to a year — when in doubt,
> call.

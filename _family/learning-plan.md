# Family & Parenthood — Learning Plan

A small, personal corner of the garden: warm, practical guides for big life
steps. It starts where our family does — **planning for a baby** — and can grow
over time (newborn care, sleep, feeding, toddler years) as we need it.

{: .important }
> These lessons are **general, evidence-based education**, drawn from public-health
> sources — **not** medical advice for any specific person. For anything about your
> own health or your baby's, follow your doctor, obstetrician, or midwife. When a
> lesson lists a warning sign, treat "when in doubt, call" as the rule.

## Phases & lessons

### Phase 1 — Starting a Family
1. **Planning for a Baby: Before, During & After Pregnancy** — the whole journey
   in three stages: preparing before conception, staying healthy and monitored
   during pregnancy, and caring for both baby and mother after delivery.

*(More lessons can be added here as the family grows.)*

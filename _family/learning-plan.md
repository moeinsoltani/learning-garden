# Family & Parenthood — Learning Plan

A warm, complete course on **becoming parents** — from the first "should we?"
conversation, through pregnancy and birth, to surviving and enjoying the first
year with a new baby. It's written for **both partners**, in plain language, with
practical checklists and honest talk about the hard parts as well as the joyful
ones.

{: .important }
> **This course is education, not medical advice.** Everything here is general,
> evidence-based information to help you feel prepared and ask good questions. It
> is **not** a substitute for your own doctor, obstetrician, or midwife — who
> know your specific situation. Whenever a lesson lists a warning sign, the rule
> is simple: **when in doubt, call.** Every lesson repeats this reminder because
> it matters.

## How this course works

Like the Canada and Leadership tracks, this is a **no-terminal** course. Each
lesson has the same shape:

- A **"Words to know"** box — plain-English definitions and pronunciations for
  the medical terms (there are many, and they get intimidating fast).
- **Concept** — the mental model and the big ideas.
- **Going Deeper** — the details, tables, and specifics.
- **Lab — Plan Together** — a scenario or checklist you work through *as a couple*,
  then reveal a model answer.
- **Further Reading** (reputable public-health & clinical sources), then
  **Checkpoint** questions and a **Homework** task, each with a hidden model
  answer.

Go at your own pace. You don't have to read it all at once — jump to the phase
that matches where you are.

## Phases & lessons

### Phase 1 — Deciding & Preparing (5 lessons)
Before pregnancy: the decision, your health, and how conception works.
1. **The whole journey at a glance** — the three stages, and the big picture
2. **Are we ready?** — the decision, expectations, and your relationship
3. **Preconception health** — the checkup, chronic conditions, medications, vaccines
4. **Nutrition & lifestyle before pregnancy** — folic acid, diet, and habits (both of you)
5. **How conception works** — cycles, ovulation, timing, and when to seek help

### Phase 2 — Pregnancy: The First Trimester (5 lessons)
Weeks 1–13: confirming, coping, and the first big decisions.
6. **Confirming pregnancy & the first visit** — tests, due date, first appointment
7. **The first trimester** — what's happening, symptoms, and coping
8. **Eating & staying safe** — prenatal vitamins, and what to avoid
9. **Early screening & tests** — the scans and genetic screening choices
10. **Miscarriage & early loss** — the reality, the support, and self-compassion

### Phase 3 — Pregnancy: Mid & Late (5 lessons)
Weeks 14–40: growing, monitoring, and getting close.
11. **The second trimester** — the "golden" months and feeling movement
12. **The third trimester** — growth, discomforts, and getting ready
13. **Pregnancy complications** — gestational diabetes, blood pressure, preeclampsia
14. **Warning signs in pregnancy** — the "call now" list and kick counts
15. **Staying well** — exercise, sleep, work, travel, and mental health

### Phase 4 — Getting Ready for Birth (4 lessons)
The last weeks of preparation.
16. **Choosing your care** — OB, midwife, doula; hospital, birth centre, or home
17. **Birth plans & pain relief** — your options, held flexibly
18. **Classes & packing** — prenatal classes and the hospital bag
19. **Setting up home & baby gear** — the essentials (and what you can skip)

### Phase 5 — Labour & Delivery (4 lessons)
The big day.
20. **Signs & stages of labour** — when it's real, and what happens
21. **Vaginal birth** — what to expect
22. **C-sections** — planned and unplanned, and recovery
23. **The golden hour** — skin-to-skin, first feed, and the partner's role

### Phase 6 — The Newborn: The Fourth Trimester (7 lessons)
The first weeks with your baby.
24. **Newborn basics** — what they can do, need, and look like
25. **Safe sleep** — reducing the risk of SIDS
26. **Breastfeeding** — latch, supply, and getting help
27. **Formula & combination feeding** — safe, confident bottle-feeding
28. **Daily care** — diapering, bathing, cord, and dressing
29. **Soothing & crying** — colic, calming, and never shaking a baby
30. **Newborn health & warning signs** — jaundice, checkups, vaccines, and red flags

### Phase 7 — Recovery & the New Family (5 lessons)
Looking after the parents, not just the baby.
31. **Mother's physical recovery** — the postpartum body and the 6-week visit
32. **Postpartum mental health** — baby blues, depression, and anxiety
33. **The partner's role** — sharing the load and spotting problems
34. **Your relationship after baby** — communication, intimacy, and teamwork
35. **Sleep, visitors & your village** — surviving, and asking for help

### Phase 8 — The First Year (5 lessons)
Growing together, month by month.
36. **Starting solids** — from milk to first foods
37. **Development & milestones** — 0–12 months, and red flags
38. **Sleep in the first year** — patterns and gentle routines
39. **Health, safety & baby-proofing** — fevers, first aid, and a safe home
40. **Back to work & childcare** — choices, transitions, and the first birthday

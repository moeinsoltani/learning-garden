# Phase 3: Communication Foundations

Communication is often the biggest differentiator between a senior engineer and a
lead — you now spend more time communicating than coding, and the quality of that
communication determines whether your ideas land, your team is aligned, and your
work gets credited. This phase covers the fundamentals: shaping messages by
audience, explaining technology to non-technical people, running meetings worth
their cost, writing docs and proposals that get read and decided, presenting work
so it lands, and leading through written/async channels. It pairs directly with
the [English for Work]({{ '/english/learning-plan.html' | relative_url }}) track,
which teaches the *language*; this phase teaches the *judgment*.

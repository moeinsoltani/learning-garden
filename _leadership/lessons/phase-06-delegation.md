# Phase 6: Delegation & Growing the Team

Delegation is how a lead scales beyond their own hands — and it's how people grow, because the work
itself is the main development tool. But delegation is hard: hoard the work and you become the
bottleneck (and starve people of growth); dump it badly and things fail. This phase builds the
skills: delegating outcomes at the right level for each person, assigning work for growth rather
than just speed, having career conversations that bend work toward where people want to go, growing
seniors into leads (your own succession), and de-risking the team's (and your own) bus factor.

The labs are scenario exercises — you write handoff conversations, plan assignments, and design
succession. Growing the team is the highest-leverage thing a lead does, and delegation is its
engine.

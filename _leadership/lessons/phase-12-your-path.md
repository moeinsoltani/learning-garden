# Phase 12: Your Path

The final phase turns from the skills to <em>you</em> — starting your leadership journey deliberately and
choosing your direction. It covers your first 90 days as a lead (the tech-lead path) and as an EM (the
bigger, peer-to-boss jump), building the support system that stops you learning leadership alone, and the
capstone: making the Lead vs. EM vs. Staff decision with real information — and keeping it revisable.

This is where the course becomes personal: your plan, your relationships, your path. The labs are your own
30/60/90 plans, your personal board of advisors, and a capstone decision memo drawing on everything you've
worked through. Leadership is a journey you'll keep re-choosing — this phase sets you up to start it well and
navigate it deliberately.

# Phase 7: Influence Without Authority

Much of a lead's impact happens through people they don't manage — peers, other teams, senior
stakeholders — where you can't rely on authority and must use **influence** instead. This is one of
the biggest shifts from individual contributor: getting important things done increasingly depends on
credibility, relationships, and persuasion, not a title. This phase builds those skills: understanding
where influence actually comes from, building buy-in for ideas, resolving conflict, aligning teams
around a direction, and driving change across an organization bigger than any team you control.

The labs are scenario exercises — you map influence networks, plan campaigns, mediate conflicts, and
design org-wide change. Influence is the currency you'll spend for the rest of your leadership career.

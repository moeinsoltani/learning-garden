# Phase 5: 1:1s, Coaching & Mentoring

Growing your people is the highest-leverage thing a lead does — a team that keeps getting better
outproduces one that stays static, and developing people is how your impact scales beyond your own
hands. This phase covers the ongoing developmental relationship: running 1:1s that actually matter
(not status meetings), listening and asking questions that surface the real problem, knowing when
to coach (grow the answer-finder) versus mentor (give the answer), mentoring engineers
deliberately, using code review as a teaching channel, and sponsorship — spending your credibility
on others' visibility.

The labs are scenario exercises — you design 1:1s, write questions, respond to real requests, and
plan growth arcs. It pairs with the [English for Work]({{ '/english/learning-plan.html' | relative_url }})
track's coaching and feedback language; this phase is the leadership *judgment*.

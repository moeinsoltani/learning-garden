# Phase 1: The Transition

The Senior → Lead move is not a promotion to "senior engineer with extra
meetings." It's a change of job: your output stops being the code you write and
becomes the outcomes your team produces. This phase handles that shift head-on —
what actually changes, the roles you're choosing between, letting go of the code
without losing your technical edge, restructuring your time around leverage, and
the uncomfortable psychology of measuring yourself differently.

Every lesson's lab is a **scenario** — a real situation you'll face — where you
write your response before revealing a model answer that explains the reasoning,
the common mistakes, and useful phrasing.

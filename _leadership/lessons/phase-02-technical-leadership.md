# Phase 2: Technical Leadership

A lead guides technical direction — not by writing most of the code, but by
helping the team make better technical decisions than they would without you.
This phase covers the technical half of leadership: setting a vision and
strategy, making architecture decisions legible (ADRs), reasoning explicitly
about trade-offs, running design reviews that grow people, driving standards
without becoming the style police, managing technical debt as a portfolio, and
answering the perennial question of how much a lead should still code.
